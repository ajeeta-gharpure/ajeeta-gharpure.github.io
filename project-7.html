Project information

  • Client: Vodafone idea
  • Project date: August, 2023
  • Project URL: Project link

  • Empowering Collaboration & Cultural Connection: Partnered with Mumbai dabbawalas to enhance Vodafone's network reliability, celebrating local culture and community by highlighting the dabbawalas' rich traditions and their integral role in Mumbai, adding authenticity to the campaign.
  • Inclusive Initiative & Local Voices: Distributed 26 phones to dabbawalas, showcasing the importance of grassroots engagement in understanding network performance. Over the course of a week, the dabbawalas carried the phones, providing invaluable insights into connectivity challenges faced by everyday users across diverse neighborhoods.
  • Local Voices: Dabbawalas carried the phones for a week, providing invaluable insights into connectivity challenges faced by everyday users in diverse neighborhoods.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Enabled real-time network testing, emphasizing Vodafone's dedication to improving user experiences through direct feedback from diverse local users.
  • Groundbreaking Representation: Felt proud to be the only woman in the on-ground activation team.
